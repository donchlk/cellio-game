# Cell.io ‚Äì Enhanced Edition

Cell.io is a lightweight single-file browser game inspired by Agar.io. [web:22]
This version is the **Enhanced Edition**, featuring more complex mechanics, better visuals, and improved AI.

---

## üöÄ Demo / Quick Start

1. Clone this repository.
2. Open `cellio_game.html` directly in your browser.
3. Enter your nickname and press **Play**.
4. Use your mouse to steer, **Space** to split, and **W** to eject mass.

---

## ‚ú® Features (Enhanced Edition)

- **Split Mechanic (Space)**: Divide your cell into two to catch smaller prey or escape enemies.
- **Mass Ejection (W)**: Shoot out mass to feed others or distract bots.
- **Improved AI**: Bots now have better target selection and roaming behaviors.
- **Minimap**: Real-time radar in the bottom right corner showing all entities.
- **Particle Effects**: Visual feedback when eating food pellets.
- **Dynamic Camera**: Smooth zoom and border clamping (no more black gaps).
- **Leaderboard**: Live Top-5 ranking of all cells.
- **Optimized Performance**: Replaced heavy shadow effects with gradients for smoother gameplay.
- **Start/Death Screens**: Proper game flow with nickname input and final score display.
